About Me

I’m Abanindranath Sahu, a 3D animator based in Kolkata, India. Inspired by films like Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse, I focus on creating expressive and believable character animation. My workflow combines Autodesk Maya for animation, V-Ray for lighting and rendering, and DaVinci Resolve and After Effects for post-production, allowing me to build complete cinematic animation projects from start to finish. Currently training at Indian VFX School, I’m continuously improving my skills in character performance, movement, and visual storytelling, with the goal of contributing to animated films, games, and creative storytelling projects.
